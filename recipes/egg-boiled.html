Yummy Soft Boiled Egg

The recipe of Yummy Soft Boiled Egg is done very quickly with minimal ingredients and preparation time. Do not be fooled by the picture as it depicts a boiled egg of the hard variety.

Ingredients

  • 500 mL water
  • 1 egg
  • salt
  • pepper
  • ketchup
  • 500 mL freezing cold water

Steps

  1. Add 500 mL water into a deep pot
  2. Turn on the heat, until it does the bubbles blub blub to the top
  3. Add the RAW egg into the pot and start a timer for 4 minutes
  4. After 30 seconds has passed you can remove the egg from the pot if you wish to eat a raw egg
  5. After the timer has finished, remove the egg from the pot and put inside a container with the freezing cold water
  6. Wait 1 minute and you can peel the egg, you can notice that the egg is cold
  7. Season the egg with salt and pepper and then eat with ketchup of any variety
