Super Crunchy Crispy Garlic

The recipe of Super Crunchy Crispy Garlic will take a little longer as to not burn the garlic which would taste bitter (not better). Surprisingly, it would look like the picture if you follow this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Lotsa Cooking Oil
  • 2 whole garlic
  • salt
  • (optional) chili oil/flakes

Steps

  1. Peel the garlic cloves with whatever black magic technique you know
  2. Mince to little tiny bits
  3. Heat a deep pan/wok as it is
  4. Add lotsa cooking oil into the pan
  5. Add the garlic with some salt
  6. Cook until it becomes lightish golden brown
  7. If you wish to add heat, add chili oil/flakes
  8. If you added the chili, pour the chili garlic into a jar with the oil
  9. Else if (no chili bruh), drain the garlic of the oil
  10. Wow so cripy :-D
