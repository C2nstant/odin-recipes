Slightly Boiled Hotdog

The recipe of Slightly Boiled Hotdog is of my own which means its the only right way to make one. Despite the presence of a hotdog bun, this recipe will not make use of one as that is blasphemous.

Ingredients

  • How many pieces of hotdog you want
  • 50 mL water
  • Cooking oil
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Look at that, this hotdog only requires 4 recipes wowie
  2. Peel and cut small even slices into the hotdog to better cook the insides
  3. Place the hotdog and the water into a hot frying pan
  4. Let the water evaporate
  5. Add the cooking oil
  6. Cook the hotdog till it becomes edible
  7. Cook more if you want it tastier
  8. It is done
  9. Serve with ketchup
